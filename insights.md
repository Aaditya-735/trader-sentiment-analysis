### Key Insights

1. Trader profitability tends to increase during Greed market sentiment periods. 
This suggests that bullish market conditions create more profitable opportunities.

2. Trade frequency increases during Fear sentiment periods, indicating that traders
may attempt to exploit volatility during uncertain market conditions.

3. A small number of traders account for the majority of total profits, showing a
skewed distribution of trading performance.

4. Traders tend to generate higher average PnL during Greed sentiment periods compared
 to Fear periods.

### Strategy Recommendations

1. Sentiment-Based Risk Management

During Fear market conditions, traders should reduce leverage and trade size 
to manage volatility risk.

2. Momentum Strategy During Greed

During Greed sentiment periods, traders can increase trading frequency 
and leverage to capture strong market trends.

3. Trader Segmentation Strategy

Platforms could identify high-performing traders and replicate their 
strategies through algorithmic trading models.
